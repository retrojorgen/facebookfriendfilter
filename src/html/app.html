Welcome to the outstanding facebook friend filter.

Is this your first time here?

Facebook friend filter is a fun little web app experiment, that lets you figure out which facebook friends you actually know, and those that could possibly be hidden or deleted.

Through a series of choices we explore your friends likes, birthdays, hometowns, locations and much more. We present you with a statement and you decide which of three possible choices has this in her or his facebook profile.

Disclaimer: No information is stored when you use this application, and no information is published back to facebook. This is for your personal use only. If you are in doubt you are free to look at the source code at github here
